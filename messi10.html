Born on June 24, 1987, Messi's journey began amidst the bustling streets and colorful neighborhoods of this bustling Argentine city. Rosario, known for its iconic architecture, bustling markets, and fervent love for the beautiful game, provided the perfect backdrop for Messi's early years, shaping his love for football and igniting a passion that would propel him to unprecedented heights on the global stage.


Considered one of the greatest football players of all time, Messi has won numerous awards during his career, including eight Ballon d'Or titles and the precious world cup in 2022.


Lionel Messi's impact extends beyond the football pitch, as he is known for his philanthropy and advocacy work. Through his Leo Messi Foundation, established in 2007, he supports access to education and healthcare for vulnerable children worldwide. Off the field, Messi maintains a relatively private personal life, prioritizing his family, including his wife Antonela Roccuzzo and their three children. Despite facing criticism and challenges throughout his career, Messi's humility, perseverance, and unparalleled talent have solidified his legacy as an icon of the sport, inspiring generations of aspiring footballers and capturing the hearts of fans worldwide.

Off-field activities

Lionel Messi, beyond his unparalleled success on the football pitch, has dedicated himself to making a positive impact off the field. Through his Leo Messi Foundation, established in 2007 alongside his family, Messi has been a beacon of hope for countless children around the world.


Leo Messi Foundation


The Leo Messi Foundation focuses on improving access to education and healthcare for vulnerable children. With a mission to ensure that every child has the opportunity to fulfill their potential, the foundation has implemented various initiatives and projects in Argentina and beyond.One of the core pillars of the Leo Messi Foundation is its commitment to education. Recognizing the transformative power of learning, Messi has supported numerous educational programs aimed at providing quality schooling for disadvantaged children.


Health care initiatives


Medical Treatment : Messi's foundation supports children with medical conditions by covering the costs of essential treatments, surgeries, and therapies, ensuring they receive the care they need to thrive.


Health Awareness Campaigns : Messi actively participates in health awareness campaigns, using his platform to raise awareness about critical health issues affecting children and advocating for preventive measures and early intervention.
